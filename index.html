Methodology — how each KPI is calculated

# KPI Formula (April deck) Source — where the data is picked from
1Variable CPHTotal Variable Cost ÷ Total JoiningsJoining Master · Actual_CPH column · Finance posts post month-end close
230-Day Attrition %(Exits in last 30 days ÷ Active HC) × 100Snowflake · CSPL_HR_DB.HRMS_PUBLIC.EMPLOYEES · DATE_OF_EXIT in last 30d ÷ EMPLOYEE_STATUS='Active'
3Offer-to-Join %(Total Joinings ÷ Total Offers Released) × 100Recruitment Metric_New · Rocket Offers tab cross-checked with V3 Joinee Tracker
4Time to Fill (d)AVG(DOJ − Requisition Open Date)Snowflake · CSPL_HR_DB.HR_ATS_PUBLIC.OFFER_LETTERS_DETAILS ⋈ REQUISITIONS (live)
5Avg TAT — Open RolesAVG(Today − Requisition Open Date) for STATUS = OpenSnowflake · CSPL_HR_DB.HR_ATS_PUBLIC.REQUISITIONS (live snapshot)
6Hike %AVG((Offered CTC − Previous CTC) ÷ Previous CTC) × 100Joining Master · Offered CTC + Last CTC columns · 149 of 217 joiners covered
7Recruiter ProductivityTotal Joinings ÷ Total Recruiter HCV3 Joinee Tracker · Recruiter column (distinct count) ÷ window joinings
8JoiningsCOUNT(DOJ in window AND status IN Active/InActive)Snowflake · CSPL_HR_DB.HRMS_PUBLIC.EMPLOYEES · system of record (replaces older Base-Live CSV view which under-counted Tech/Product)
